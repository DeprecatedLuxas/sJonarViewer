### Prerequisites

Before you continue, ensure you have installed the follwing requirements:

* You have installed [NodeJS](https://nodejs.org/en/download/) on your system 
* You have installed [mkcert](https://github.com/FiloSottile/mkcert/blob/master/README.md) on your system to make the ssl certificates (You can also use another certificate maker)
